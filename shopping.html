Welcome to our care foundation

Child Care Foundation a registered charitable trust ( Reg: No : 98/2012 ) aims at creating a long and strong relationship between individuals and business to generate funds for the well being of the homeless children. Situated in Chennai, Child Care foundation works closely the children taking care of their day to day needs. With your help and support we can change the way these children live and provide the children a new meaning to their lives. Child Care Foundation needs your support to help us for homeless children and old age people.

Participate

At Child Care Foundation we provide a warm and safe environment for the children which enables and helps the developing children to achieve their full potential by proper food and sanitation facilities. Our foundation also conducts various donation programs through which we try to eradicate the hunger condition of the needy children. Child Care Foundation also organizes educational & motivation programs which help the children in terms of Social awareness, the need to education, health and relationship.
